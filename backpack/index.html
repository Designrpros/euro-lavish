    🎒 Backpack & Packing Guide

    The ultimate packing list for your European adventure

    🎒 Backpack Essentials

    Clothing

    Item Notes
    🧥 Light jacket Layers for changing weather
    👕 T-shirts 3-4 breathable tops
    👖 Pants/Shorts 2 pants, 1-2 shorts
    🧦 Socks 4-5 pairs
    🩳 Underwear 5-6 pairs
    🧢 Hat Sun protection
    🧣 Scarf For colder days

    Footwear

    Item Notes
    👟 Walking shoes Comfortable for cities
    🩴 Flip flops Hostel/Beach
    👢 Waterproof shoes Rainy seasons

    Toiletries

    Item Notes
    🪥 Toothbrush & Paste
    🧴 Shampoo/Soap Travel size
    ☀️ Sunscreen Essential summer
    💊 First Aid Kit Basic supplies
    🧻 Quick-dry towel Lightweight

    Tech

    Item Notes
    📱 Phone With offline maps
    🔌 Power adapter EU plug (Type C/F)
    🔋 Power bank For long days
    🎧 Headphones Noise cancelling
    📷 Camera Optional

    Documents

    Item Notes
    🛂 Passport Valid 6+ months
    💳 Bank cards Notify bank abroad
    🏥 Travel insurance Essential!
    🏨 Booking confirmations Print/digital
    📄 Emergency contacts

    💡 Pro Tips

    Backpack Size

    • 40-50L - Perfect for weekend trips
    • 50-65L - Extended trips (1-3 months)
    • Carry-on - Most airlines: 40L max

    Weight Tips

    • Pack light! Max 10kg recommended
    • Roll clothes to save space
    • Wear heaviest items on plane

    Money Saving

    • Bring reusable water bottle
    • Buy food at supermarkets
    • Use local SIM or eSIM

    🌧️ Seasonal Packing

    Summer (Jun-Aug)

    • Light breathable clothing
    • Sunscreen
    • Sunglasses
    • Small daypack

    Winter (Dec-Feb)

    • Warm layers
    • Thermal underwear
    • Good waterproof jacket
    • Warm boots

    Shoulder (Mar-May, Sep-Nov)

    • Mix of both
    • Always bring a jacket
    • Layers are key
